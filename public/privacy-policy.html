Privacy Policy

Simple Calc does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information. We only use third-party tools for basic analytics and advertising.

We use Google AdSense to display ads. Google may use cookies and personalized data to serve relevant ads. Learn more at Google Ads Policy.

Analytics & Session Recording

We use Microsoft Clarity to understand user behavior. Clarity records anonymized user interactions such as clicks, scrolls, and navigation patterns. This helps us improve user experience. Clarity does not collect personal data, and all information is processed under GDPR and CCPA compliance. Learn more at clarity.microsoft.com.
